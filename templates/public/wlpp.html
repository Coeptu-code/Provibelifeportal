How It Works · White Label Overview

From the harvest
to your shelf.

Pro Vibe Life offers a complete white label solution for brands ready to enter the premium Shilajit market. Fully integrated harvest-to-shelf — a finished, tested, retail-ready product under your brand name.

01 / Product✦

100% Pure Resin Tablets

Premium formula — ~60% fulvic acid, ~14% humic acid, 200mg per tablet, 60 tablets per box.

02 / Supply⌂

Harvest-to-Shelf Integration

Direct partnerships with harvesters and our Russian partner lab. No middlemen, full traceability.

03 / Quality⌬

Independent Lab Testing

Every batch tested for heavy metals, microbiological safety, and potency. Full COAs available.

04 / Compliance✓

All Standards Met

Prop 65, USP <232>, ICH Q3D, and GMP certified — ready for US retail shelves.

05 / Branding◐

Your Brand, Your Product

Sell under your own brand name. We supply the product; you own the customer relationship.

06 / Scale⤢

Scalable from Day One
